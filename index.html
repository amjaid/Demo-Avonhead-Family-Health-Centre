About Our Clinic

Providing compassionate, comprehensive healthcare for the whole family in a warm and welcoming environment.

🏥

Your Health, Our Priority

At Avonhead Family Health Centre, we believe in healthcare that puts people first. Our dedicated team of experienced general practitioners and support staff work together to provide personalised medical care for every member of your family — from infants to seniors.

Conveniently located in the heart of Avonhead, Christchurch, our modern facility is equipped with the latest medical technology to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. We are proud to be an accredited General Practice meeting the highest standards of care.

  • Patient-centred care approach
  • Modern, well-equipped facilities
  • Experienced & friendly team
  • ACC registered providers

Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare services tailored to every stage of life.

General Health Check

Comprehensive health assessments, blood tests, and preventive screenings for all ages.

👩

Women's Health

👶

Children's Health
